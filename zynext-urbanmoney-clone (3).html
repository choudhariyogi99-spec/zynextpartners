We're Here to Answer All Your Questions

From refinancing to reducing your interest, we have the answers right here.

What is ZYNEXT Partners?
+

ZYNEXT Partners is a fintech loan aggregator that assists millions in achieving their financial goals. Since 2023, ZYNEXT Partners has changed how lending works by offering personalised loan options from various banks and NBFCs. We guide clients through every loan process step — from selecting the right loan to handling paperwork, legal support, and disbursing the loan.

How does ZYNEXT Partners work?
+

ZYNEXT Partners simplifies borrowing by connecting individuals with various financial products including personal loans, home loans, business loans, and loans against property. Through our digital platform, users can compare loan options from over 35 lenders, ensuring they find the best fit for their financial needs. We offer tools like EMI calculators and eligibility checkers to assist users in making informed decisions.

Is ZYNEXT Partners safe and secure?
+

ZYNEXT Partners is a reputable fintech company partnering with over 35 trusted banks and financial institutions. With advanced technology, we safeguard user data and transactions, following strict security protocols to ensure privacy. For every credit score check, ZYNEXT Partners requires your consent, ensuring your information remains private and is never shared without permission.

Does ZYNEXT Partners store my bank login credentials?
+

No, ZYNEXT Partners does not store your bank login credentials. Users create their own accounts, and login credentials are strictly private. The platform prioritises user privacy and security, ensuring that sensitive information remains confidential and is not retained on our systems.

How do I become a DSA Partner with ZYNEXT?
+

Becoming a DSA Partner with ZYNEXT Partners is completely free and takes less than 2 minutes. Simply register on our platform, add your customers, and start earning commissions up to ₹1 Lakh per month. No prior experience is required — we provide complete training and support.

Does ZYNEXT Partners support all Indian banks?
+

Yes, ZYNEXT Partners collaborates with over 35 banks, including major public and private sector banks and non-banking financial companies (NBFCs) in India. Our network includes HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, SBI, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, Bajaj Finserv, IDFC FIRST Bank, and many more leading financial institutions.
